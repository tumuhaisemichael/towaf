Who We Are

Restoring Hope, Rebuilding Lives

Our Mission

To restore hope and rebuild lives in post-war and crisis-affected communities in Africa, starting with Tigray, Ethiopia. We focus on healing and holistic recovery.

Our Vision

To turn post-conflict communities into resilient, self-sustaining centers of prosperity where every individual has the opportunity to thrive with dignity.

Our Origin

Founded in Roseville, CA by Dr. Fetien Abay Abera, an Ethiopian professor and humanitarian dedicated to transformative change.

Our Journey

The TOWAF Story

The Catalyst

During the 2021 Tigray war, Dr. Fetien led Mekelle University through siege conditions, coordinating food aid and ensuring student safety.

Refugee for Refugee

Witnessing refugee trauma in Sudan inspired the R4R initiative, supporting PhD students and refugees through education and technology.

Mentorship & Education

Student feedback: "You helped us as a torch in bringing light during our darkest time" became the driving force for the foundation.

Formalization

TOWAF established as a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit in California, dedicated to education and empowerment across Africa.

What We Do

Core Focus Areas

Our holistic approach covers immediate relief to long-term sustainable development across six key departments.

Survivor Support

Holistic aid for women affected by conflict, providing housing, business grants, and psychosocial care to rebuild dignity.

Education & R4R

Refugee for Refugee initiative helping displaced scholars and students continue their education through scholarships and SAT support.

Capacity Building

Vocational training and leadership development to foster self-sustaining communities and economic independence.

Tech Infrastructure

Bridging the digital divide by donating computers and strengthening exam infrastructure for students in need.
